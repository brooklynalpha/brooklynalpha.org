Location

The Venue

Located in the heart of Gowanus, The Cabinet magazine is more Brooklyn than the Barclay's Center. Situated in a late 19th century building that houses not only The Cabinet and Brooklyn Alpha, but also art collectives and other creative ventures, 300 Nevins Street gives that unmistakable Brooklyn charm making it a perfect location for hosting our small, friendly build-a-thon.
http://cabinetmagazine.org

Getting Here

The Cabinet is located at 300 Nevins St., in the heart of Gowanus. The easiest way to get there is by subway.

  • 5 minute walk from the Union St. stop on the R train
  • 10 minute walk from the Carroll St. stop on the F G trains
