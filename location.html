The Cabinet

http://cabinetmagazine.org

When we were choosing a location to host Brooklyn Alpha, we decided early on that we wanted to have it at a venue that has that distinct "Brooklyn" feel to it. Cabinet magazine's rental space is about as Brooklyn as Biggie. The building was home to the American Packing Box factory from 1915-1954. Nowadays, its neighborhood – Gowanas – is quickly becoming home to a slew of art spaces, something else and a handful of other creative ventures by motivated Brooklynites. You're gonna love it!

Getting here

The Cabinet is located in the heart of Gowanus, at 300 Nevins St. at the intersection of Nevins St. and Union St., and is easily reached by subway.

  • 5 minute walk from the Union St. stop on the R train
  • 10 minute walk from the Carroll St. stop on the F/G trains
