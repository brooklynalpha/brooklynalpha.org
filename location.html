The Cabinet

http://cabinetmagazine.org

When we were choosing a location to host Brooklyn Alpha, we decided early on that we wanted to have it at a venue that has that distinct "Brooklyn" feel to it. Cabinet magazine's rental space is about as Brooklyn as Biggie. The building was home to the National Packing Box factory from 1889-1936 and had a slew of industrial tenants before becoming working space for artists. Nowadays, the entire neighborhood is quickly becoming home to a slew of art collectives, performance spaces and a handful of other creative ventures by motivated Brooklynites. You're gonna love it!

Getting here

The Cabinet is located at 300 Nevins St., in the heart of Gowanus and is easily reached by subway.

  • 5 minute walk from the Union St. stop on the R train
  • 10 minute walk from the Carroll St. stop on the F G trains
